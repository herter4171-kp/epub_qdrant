Semantic Versus Dense Results:
* You will always be given an even number of results
* Distribution
    * The first half of them will be sparse vectors
    * The second half of them will be dense vectors
    * Do not compare scores between sparse and dense vectors
* Utility
    * Sparse results are good when the prompt contains exact terms that matter such as but not limited to:
        * Proper nouns
        * Code snippets
        * Model names
        * IDs
        * Technial jargon
    * Dense results are useful when the prompt is vague or paraphrased and expresses without significant use of specific terms
